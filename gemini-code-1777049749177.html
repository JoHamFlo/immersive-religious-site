4. Dismantling Misconceptions

Myth: Islam was spread by the sword [cite: 230]

Truth: There is no compulsion in religion (Surah 2:256). Many regions like Indonesia adopted Islam through trade[cite: 231, 232].

Myth: All Muslims are Arabs [cite: 233]

Truth: Only 20% of Muslims are Arabs; the largest populations are in Indonesia, Pakistan, India, and Bangladesh[cite: 233, 234].
